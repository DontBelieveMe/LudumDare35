LD35: A Shape Odyssey:
---------------------

Use [A], [D] to move forwards and backwards
Press [Q] to switch between human and bird forms.

When in HUMAN form:
    - PRESS [SPACE] to jump
When in BIRD form
    - HOLD [Space] to fly

Press [Escape] to pause then [Escape] again to resume play.

Yellow flags are checkpoints

Notes:
Very occasionly the player will not go through a 1 block wide tunnel. 
If this happens jump a bit and it normally goes through
